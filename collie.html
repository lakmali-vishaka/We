            History of collie

      the collie dog is originaed as a shephered's dog in the highlands of northern scotland.there were two different kind of collie dogs. they are rough collie and smooth-coated collie.collies became show dogs and first exhibited in 1860 at the Bermingham,England,dog show.The american cannel club recognized the breed in 1885 and in 1886 the breed's standard height ,weight and physical characteristics were estimblished. In the 20 century,collie were less frequently herders and more often family dogs.Today collie make a wonderful family pet.
